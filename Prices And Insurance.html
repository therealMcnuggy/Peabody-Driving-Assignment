Test Pricing

Normal prices

Here at Peabody Driving we try to offer a wide variety of practical lesson options. Our most popular option are our pay as you go lesssons meaning that you only need to buy the amount that you need to pass and you have no commitment. These cost around £50 per lesson. However we also offer many packages at a discount per lesson to pay as you go. Our most popular package is the 10-lesson package, this includes 10 one-hour lessons costing around £40 per lesson. We also offer a 20-lesson package for around £35 per lesson and a 30-lesson package for £30 per lesson if you require it.
We also offer options for you to be able to take your teory driving tests starting from around £60 per test.



Special packages

At Peabody Driving we also offer something we call special packges. These include everything you need to pass your driving test such as practical tests and theory tests. For the midrange packages you also get books with all of the information you need for your tests, as well as personal help and tutoring from me with some of our more premium packages. The price of these packages range from £300 for the student pack and up to £1060 for the most premium pack.
The different special packs include the:

  • Student pack £300: 10 lessons and a theory test.
  • Regular pack £500: 10 lessons, theory test as well as a book.
  • Regular pack+ £800: 20 lessons, theory test and a book.
  • Plus pack £1000: 30 lessons, theory test and a book.
  • Premium pack £1150: 30 lessons, theory test, a book and help from me whenever you need it.



Insurance Information

Insurance price

Each of the one hour practical lessons that you purchase through either a package or individually on its own come included with free insurance that you will sign for when you checkout.
This insurance covers you for anything that migh happen when you are completing the practical driving tests.
